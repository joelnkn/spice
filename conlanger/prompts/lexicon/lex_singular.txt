       • list them as full word forms, and
       • do NOT segment or expose the affix inside the word form.
   - For each derivational affix in the inventory, include at least one lexical item whose meaning
     reflects that derivational function (e.g., an agentive noun, a diminutive noun, etc.),
     but NEVER include the affix itself as a standalone form.

5. Coverage
   Include at least:
   - Basic nouns (persons, objects, locations, abstract concepts).
   - Basic verbs (common actions, processes, states).
   - Basic adjectives (qualities, states).
   - Function words needed by the typology:
       • pronouns,
       • adpositions (prepositions/postpositions, depending on the feature vector),
       • basic question words,
       • demonstratives or determiners if appropriate.

- form: the full conlang word form (lemma, uninflected).
